The BMW i8 is a plug-in hybrid sports car developed by BMW. The i8 is part of BMW's electrified fleet and is marketed under the BMW i sub-brand. The production version of the BMW i8 was unveiled at the 2013 Frankfurt Motor Show, and was released in Germany in June 2014.[10] Deliveries to retail customers in the U.S. began in August 2014.[11] A roadster variant was launched May 2018.[12] Production ended in June 2020.
